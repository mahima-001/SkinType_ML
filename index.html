About SkiScan

SkiScan is an advanced skin type prediction system that uses machine learning algorithms to analyze your unique skin characteristics and provide personalized skincare recommendations.

How It Works

1

Answer Questions

Complete our comprehensive questionnaire about your skin and lifestyle

2

Validate Analysis

Our Random Forest model analyzes your responses using patterns from 100,000+ skin profiles

3

Get Results

Receive your skin type prediction with confidence scores and personalized recommendations

The Science Behind It

Our model is trained on a comprehensive dataset of 100,000 skin profiles, considering factors like age, gender, water intake, weather conditions, oiliness levels, and various skin conditions. The Random Forest algorithm provides high accuracy predictions while explaining the reasoning behind each recommendation.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the skin type prediction?

Our Validate model achieves high accuracy rates by analyzing patterns from over 100,000 skin profiles. However, results should be considered as guidance and may vary based on individual factors.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes, we take privacy seriously. All analysis is done locally in your browser, and no personal information is stored or transmitted to external servers.

Can I retake the analysis?

Absolutely! You can retake the analysis as many times as you want. We recommend retaking it if your skin conditions change significantly.

Should I consult a dermatologist?

While our Validate provides valuable insights, it's always recommended to consult with a qualified dermatologist for professional medical advice, especially for serious skin conditions.

What skin types can be detected?

Our system can identify four main skin types: Normal, Dry, Oily, and Sensitive. Each comes with specific care recommendations and product suggestions.
